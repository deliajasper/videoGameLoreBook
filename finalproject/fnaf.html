Five Nights at Freddy's

The Five Nights at Freddy's (FNAF) lore is one of the most complicated in recent history.


Lore TLDR:

This guy named William Afton kills kids, they possess animatronic suits and then his son Michael goes on a quest to release their souls


Lore Overview


There are many different interpretations of this lore, the one the author is basing this retelling off of is Game Theory's theories about the lore. Click here to see a playlist of all of their theories about FNAF.


It all starts with two friends, William Afton and Henry Emily. They are the owners and co-founders of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. William has 3 children: Michael, Elizabeth, and Evan. Herny only has one: Charlotte (Charlie). They also both have wives.

The First Location / Charlie's Death

The first pizza restaurant ever opened was Fredbear's Family Diner. This location was extremely popular. There were only two animatronics, Fredbear and Spring Bonnie. These animatronics are springlock animatronics, this means they also double as suits for employees to wear. However, these suits are very dangerous and lock up with any moisture, killing the wearer. At this location, in order to protect his daughter, Henry created the marionette animatronic. However, one day the puppet malfunctioned and it got stuck. That day, Charlie (Henry's Daughter) was killed by William Afton. The puppet then finds Charlie's body, then Charlie's spirit possesses the puppet. This is William's first murder.

After Charlie's death, Fredbear's Family Diner was sold to new owners, a new location is then opened: Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. This is the location seen in FNAF 2.

The Bite of '83

Soon after Charlie's death, the Bite of '83 takes place. This is when Evan Afton is forcibly put in the mouth of a springlock animatronic while crying, causing it to lock up, crushing his head and killing him. This finally causes Fredbear's Family Diner to close.

The Missing Children's Incident

In the new location, The Missing Children's Incident occurs. This is when William Afton in the Spring Bonnie suit, lures 5 children into the back of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. He then kills them. He stuffs their bodies into 4 work in progress suits: Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy. He then puts the last child into the old Fredbear suit. These children's spirits then go on to possess the suits they are stuffed into.

The Victims of the Missing Children's Incident

  • Gabriel - Freddy
  • Jeremy - Bonnie
  • Susie - Chica (The first to die)
  • Fritz - Foxy
  • Cassidy - Fredbear

Cassidy later becomes known as "Golden Freddy". Golden Freddy has two spirits inside of him: Cassidy (the Missing Children's Incident victim) and Evan Afton (William's son who was killed in the Bite of '83).

Circus Baby's Pizza World

After the Missing Children’s Incident, Afton built a new animatronic, Circus Baby. She was built to capture and kill children. He opens a new location (a sister location) Circus Baby’s Pizza World. Elizabeth Afton, William's Daughter, was killed by Circus Baby after being lured by ice cream. This causes Circus Baby’s to close on its opening day

After the closing of Circus Baby’s Pizza world William went forward with the rental service. The animatronics were put into storage, but were available to the public to rent out. This turns into the warehouse that Sister Location takes place in

The Second Missing Children's Incident / The Bite of '87

In 1987, they reopen a Freddy Fazbear’s Pizza with new and improved animatronics. This is the FNAF 2 location, FNAF 2 is a prequel. The toy animatronics are very secure, they have an advanced facial recognition system

In this new location William kills 5 more kids them going on to possess the Toy Animatronics, Mangle, and Balloon Boy(?). We do not know the identities of these kids as this is not 100% cannon, but most theorists are sure that William killed 5 more kids

This is the famed “Bite of ‘87” that Phone Guy in FNAF 1 talks about. After the Second Missing Children’s Incident the Toy Animatronics become more aggressive towards adults. The animatronics end up attacking the daytime security guard, biting off his frontal lobe.

After the Bite of ‘87 the FNAF 2 location shuts down. A few years later in 1993 they open a new location, the FNAF 1 location. They have the original Work in Progress animatronics possessed by the kids killed in the first Missing Children’s Incident. No one comes, then it shuts down.

William Afton's Death

A while after the FNAF 1 location shuts down William goes back to the location and dismantling the animatronics. He sets the souls free, and they go after him. He runs into the Spring Bonnie Suit, but it is raining. The springlocks fail and he gets crushed in the Spring Bonnie suit, dying.

Michael Afton: The Beginning (Sister Location)

While this is all going on, Michael takes a job at the warehouse seen in Sister Location goaded on by his father before his death. There, he discovers that his sister is inhabiting the animatronic Baby and that his father killed a bunch of kids. He then is determined to set the souls of the kids free

Michael, while working at the Sister Location location gets “scooped”. This means he gets his insides ripped out of him because this machine is used to rip remnant out of animatronics. He gets used as a skin suit by Enard, who is an amalgamation of all of the Sister Location animatronics

Michael's Quest

Michael lives even without Enard inside of him somehow. Michael is the second ”Purple Guy”. He gets jobs at both the 1987 Location and the 1993 Location using the names of the original Missing Children's Incident victims. However, he gets fired for tampering with the animatronics and “odor”

Fazbear Frights

After William dies in the suit in the backroom it gets walled off. Years later some people want to make a horror attraction out of an old Freddy Fazbear’s Pizza. They open up the old back room, setting William free, he is the main antagonist of FNAF 3 as Springtrap. Fazbear Frights is the FNAF 3 location

Michael then gets a job at Fazbear Frights. At the end of FNAF 3 Michael tries to kill his dad once and for all by burning Fazbear Frights to the ground. However, this again does not kill William

Henry's Return

In FNAF 6, Henry opens a pizzeria as a last-ditch effort to get all of the animatronics, William, and Michael in one place. The animatronics include Springtrap, Scrap Baby (who got kicked out of Enard), Lefty (the puppet), and Molten Freddy. He then burns the pizzeria to the ground, killing Michael, letting the animatronics’ souls free

The Timeline Split

The vengeful spirit of Cassidy (Golden Freddy) keeps William alive in order to torture him in a never-ending nightmare. This is Ultimate Custom Night. However, this is only half of William’s story

There are now 2 William Aftons. When a game developer years later decides to capitalize on the horror that was Fazbear Entertainment creating a video game series . This is The Freddy Fazbear Virtual Experience. The developer used old code found on old Freddy Fazbear’s computers, awakening the consciousness of William Afton

Glichtrap and Vanny

Glitchtrap is William’s consciousness coded into The Freddy Fazbear Virtual Experience. He takes shape as a yellow rabbit, reminiscent of Springtrap. He corrupts the beta testers turning them into William Aftons in training.

In FNAF VR Vanny is introduced. She is a beta tester that was corrupted by William. She also appears in FNAF Security Breach.

Security Breach

In this game, you play as Gregory, a robot child. In this game, (in the true ending) you descend down into the basement and find the remains of the FNAF 6 restaurant.

Michael Afton is notably absent in this game, as you do not play as him. But he is the soul that possesses Glamrock Freddy as he is trying to protect Gregory (most likely possessed by Evan).

Character Descriptions


Freddy Fazbear and Variations
  • Freddy Fazbear - FNAF 1 Freddy, the orignial work in progresas suit
  • Withered Freddy - a decayed version of the original Freddy
  • Toy Freddy - the FNAF 2 Freddy, new and improved
  • Shadow Freddy - a shadow version of Freddy with large, menacing teeth
  • Fredbear/Golden Freddy - one of the original two animatronics, a golden bear
  • Phantom Freddy - a hallucination of Freddy
  • Nightmare Freddy - a decayed version of Freddy with large claws and teeth
  • Nightmare - a monstrous version of Shadow Freddy
  • Funtime Freddy - a white and pink version of Freddy, appears in Sister Location
  • Rockstar Freddy - an updated version of the Toy Freddy
  • Lefty - a blakc version of Rockstar Freddy that the puppet possesses
  • Yenndo - a bare endoskeleton
  • Molten Freddy - a scrap version of Freddy, what used to be Ennard
  • Glamrock Freddy - a new version of Freddy, appears in Security Breach
Bonnie and Variations
  • Bonnie - FNAF 1 Bonnie, the original work in progress suit
  • Withered Bonnie - a decayed version of the original Bonnie
  • Toy Bonnie - the FNAF 2 Bonnie, new and improved
  • Shadow Bonnie - a shadow version of Bonnie
  • Nightmare Bonnie - a monstrous version of Bonnie
  • Rockstar Bonnie - an updated version of the orignial Bonnie
Chica and Variations
  • Chica - FNAF 1 Chica, the original work in progress suit
  • Withered Chica - a decayed version of the original Chica
  • Toy Chica - the FNAF 2 Chica, new and improved
  • Phantom Chica - a shadow version of the original Chica
  • Nightmare Chica - a monstrous version of Bonnie
  • Rockstar Chica - an updated version of the orignial Chica
  • Glamrock Chica - a new version of Chica, appears in Security Breach
Foxy / Mangle and Variations
  • Foxy - FNAF 1 Foxy, the original work in progress suit
  • Withered Foxy - a decayed version of the original Foxy
  • Mangle - appears in FNAF 2, a mangled version of the Foxy animatronic recolored
  • Phantom Foxy - a shadow version of the original Foxy
  • Phantom Mangle - a shadow version of Mangle
  • Nightmare Foxy - a monstrous version of Foxy
  • Funtime Foxy - a white and pink version of Foxy, appears in Sister Location
  • Lolbit - a recolored, disembodied Foxy head
  • Rockstar Foxy - an updated version of the orignial Foxy
Springtrap and Variations
  • Spring Bonnie - one of the original two animatronics, a golden rabbit
  • Springtrap - a weathered version of Spring Bonnie, possessed by William Afton
  • Scraptrap - a mangled version of Springtrap, you can see William Afton's decaying body inside it
  • Glitchtrap - a digital virus of William's consciousness, recruits followers
  • Burntrap - the last remains of William's body in Security Breach
Other Animatronics
  • The Puppet - a marionette possessed by Charlie Emily, Henry's daughter
  • Balloon Boy - another Toy Animatronic
  • Circus Baby - a clown animtronic possessed by William Afton's daughter Elizabeth
  • Scrap Baby - a mangled version of Baby, the other half of Ennard
  • Ballora - a human-like ballerina, theorized to be possessed by William's unnamed wife
  • Ennard - an amalgamation of the Sister Location animatronics, possessed Michael Afton's body for a time
  • Montgomery Gator - a violent alligator animatronic, appears to be Bonnie's replacement, appears in Security Breach
  • Roxanne Wolf - an egotistical wolf animatronic, appears in Security Breach
Human Characters
  • William Afton - a co-founder of Fazbear Entertainment, a serial killer, and the possessor of Springtrap, also known as the first Purple Guy
  • Henry Emily -the other co-founder of Fazbear Entertainment, does not participate in the murders and eventually condemns William to hell
  • Elizabeth Afton - the daughter of William, possesses Baby
  • Michael Afton - the son of William, tries to free the souls of his father's victims, the second Purple Guy
  • The Crying Child (Evan Afton) - William's other son, killed in the Bite of '83
  • Vanny - a human follower of Glitchtrap (William Afton)
  • Vanessa - the security guard of Security Breach, theorized to be Vanny
  • Gregory - the protagonist of Security Breach, theorized to be a robot
