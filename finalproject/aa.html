Ace Attorney


The Pheonix Wright Saga

Ace Attorney


Lore TLDR:

This guy named William Afton kills kids, they possess animatronic suits and then his son Michael goes on a quest to release their souls


Lore Overview (The Non-Spinoffs)


Pre-Pheonix Wright: Ace Attorney

When in elementary school, Pheonix is put on trial for stealing Miles Edgeworth's lunch money. Edgeworth defends him, proving his innocence

Pheonix and Miles then become best friends. However, Miles' father is murdered, leading him to move away.

In college, Pheonix dates Dahlia. An ex of hers tells Pheonix that she is dangerous and is then found dead the next morning. Pheonix is put on trial for the murder and is defended by Mia Fey and Dahlia gets convicted of the murder.

Pheonix Wright: Ace Attorney

Pheonix decides to work for Mia after he graduates, and wins his first case. Then, she is murdered. Maya, her sister, is put on trial for her murder and Pheonix defends her. Miles Edgeworth returns as a prosecutor (an undefeated one) but Pheonix defends Maya and convicts the real murderer.

Later, Miles is put on trial for the murder of another defense attorney. Manfred vin Karma is the prosecutor in this case, Edgeworth's old mentor. This case is connected to the DL-6 incident in which Edgeworth's father was murdered. Edgeworth believes that he somehow killed his father, but Pheonix proves that Edgeworth did not kill Robert Hammond or his father, and that Manfred Von Karma did. Disgraced, Miles leaves the city.

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney - Justice for All

After the events of the first game, Mia moves back to her home village to continue her training as a spirit medium. While training, Maya is accused of killing someone while under the influence of a spirit. Forced to defend her again, Pheonix runs into a new prosecutor, Franziska Von Karma (Manfred's Daughter). She is also the foster sister of Miles Edgeworth, Edgeworth is believed to have committed suicide. Pheonix proves her innocence. After this case, Pheonix takes Maya's cousin Pearl on as an apprentice.

Maya is then kidnapped by Shelly de Killer, who threatens to kill her if Pheonix does not get her client acquitted. De Killer stages an assassination of Franziska Von Karma, who is show and survives, but is unable to serve as prosecutor on this case. None other than Miles Edgeworth then shows up to be the prosecutor. Pheonix discovers that de Killer's client hired them to kill the person who was murdered. Pheonix then has to decide whether to convict de Killer's client at the risk of Maya's life. While Pheonix tries to prolong the trial, Edgeworth offers to help him because he has also been trying to convict de Killer. Using a tape that the client recorded as blackmail, Pheonix proves to de Killer that their client was going to blackmail them. The client pleads guilty, Maya is released.

Edgeworth reveals that his absense was an attempt to find the "answer" to what it means to be a prosecutor, and he and Pheonix reconcile. Franziska pledges to renounce her position, but Edgeworth confronts her and she recognizes she has to find the same answer he did, so she returns to Germany.

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney - Trials and Tribulations

This game starts with a flashback to when Pheonix was dating Dahlia and he gets accused of her ex's murder.

The next flashback case depicts Mia's first case where she is defending an inmate accused of killing policewoman Valerie Hawthorne. The prosecutor in this case is Edgeworth, it being his first case. Mia eventually learns the truth that Dahlia had killed Valerie.

In the last case, Pheonix is visiting a resort with Maya and Pearl when a guest is murdered. While looking for Maya, Pheonix falls into a river and contracts pneumonia. This forces Edgeworth to fill in as the defense attorney, facing off against Franziska Von Karma to keep his client, Iris, from getting convicted. When Pheonix returns, he explains that he found out that the murder victim was really Maya's long-lost mother Misty Fey. Her death was the result of a plan from her sister Morgan to kill Maya with the help of the newly-executed Dahlia who is Iris's twin. Pheonix then learns during the trial that not only was Dahlia impersonating Iris, but she was using Maya's body to do so. Dahlia is then exorcised from Maya and the real Iris is declared innocent.

Apollo Justice: Ace Attorney

Before this game, Pheonix Wright was disbarred because he was set up to present false evidence during trial. Apollo Justice becomes his mentee along with his adopted daughter Trucy.

Apollo and his mentor Kristoph Gavin are called to represent Pheonix Wright after he is accused of murdering someone over a rigged game of poker. During the trial, Pheonix names Kristoph as a witness, and Apollo is able to prove that he is the real murderer. Pheonix offers to help Apollo start his own practice out of Trucy's talent agency (The Wright Anything Agency).

Seven years earlier, Pheonix was tricked into using false evidence while representing his client Zak Gramarye which cost him his reputation and job. Zak then disappeared, leaving Trucy in Pheonix's care. Throughout his disbarred years, Pheonix uncovers the truth that Apollo and Trucy are half-siblings gifted with the power of perception.

During the trial for a case in which Apollo's client Vera is accused of poisoning her father, Kristoph is summoned to testify. He reveals that he had been Zak's original attorney before Pheonix but was fired. Kristoph was the one who had requested the creation of the fake evidence. Apollo then accuses him of setting up Phoenix with the false evidence before attempting to kill everyone related to the case out of fear of being found out.

Vera is found not guilty, and with his name cleared Pheonix applies for readmission to the bar.

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney - Dual Destinies

This game takes place about a year after Apollo Justice, Pheonix has retaken the bar exam and is once again a defense attorney. He then hires a new lawyer Athena Cykes to assist Apollo.

After being injured while investigating a case, Apollo decides to take a leave of absense from the agency to investigate on his own. Athena is then arrested on suspicion of a murder

Phoenix's investigation makes him to look into an incident seven years ago, in which Athena's mother Metis was killed and Blackquill was convicted for the murder. Blackquill's sister Aura takes several people hostage, including Phoenix's adopted daughter Trucy, demanding that a re-trial of the case be held to convict Athena for Metis's murder. Together with chief prosecutor Miles Edgeworth, Phoenix deduces that a third party was responsible for the murder, proving Athena's and Blackquill's innocence.

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney - Spirit of Justice

Pheonix travels to the village of Khura'in to visit Maya Fey, who is almost done with her spirit medium training and is on her way to becoming a master. Phoenix discovers that Khura'in is very prejudiced against defense attorneys. Under the Defense Culpability Act, if a criminal is found guilty, anyone who defends or aids them will be given the same punishment. Pheonix successfully defends his client despite this and learns of the Defiant Dragons, a rebel group fighting against the current legal system.

Back home, Athena and Apollo are defending Trucy after she is accused of murder. When Pheonix reunites with Maya, he must defend her when she is accused of murdering high priest Tahrust Inmee, who actually committed suicide to protect his wife Beh'leeb after she accidentally killed one of their disciples. She is proven innocent.

After many trials, the current queen of Khura'in is dethroned after it is proven that she has no claim to the throne. This means that all laws enacted under her rule are nullified including the Defense Culpability Act. Apollo decides to stay in Khura'in to help rebuild the kingdom's legal system.

Character Descriptions


Protagonists
  • Pheonix Wright - the protagonist, a defense attorney
  • Miles Edgeworth - the deuteragonist, a prosecutor
  • Mia Fey - Pheonix's mentor
  • Maya Fey - Mia's little sister, helps Pheonix in his investigations
  • Apollo Justice - a new attorney mentored by Pheonix
Recurring Characters
  • Dick Gumshoe - the detective in the Ace Attorney series
  • Larry Butz - Pheonix's childhood best friend
  • Trucy Wright - appears later in the series as Pheonix's adopted daughter
  • Manfred Von Karma - a renowned prosecutor who never lost a case until the case of Robert Hammond, Edgeworth's mentor
  • Franziska Von Karma - Manfred's daughter, a violet prosecutor
  • Kristoph Gavin - mentor of Apollo Justice
  • Klavier Gavin - Kristoph's younger brother, a prosecutor
  • Pearl Fey - Maya's cousin
  • Dahlia Hawthorne - Pheonix's ex-girlfriend
  • Athena Cykes - a new hire at the Wright Anything Agency to assist Apollo
